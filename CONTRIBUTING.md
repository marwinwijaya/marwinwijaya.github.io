# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in improving this website. Please follow these steps:

1. Open an issue to discuss your proposed changes (content, design, or fixes).
2. Fork the repository and create a feature branch.
3. Make your changes with clear commits and concise messages.
4. Submit a pull request describing the changes and why they are needed.

## Content Updates

- Keep wording concise and professional.
- Ensure new links work and use HTTPS where possible.
- For images/screenshots, place files in `images/` and reference them with clear names.

By participating you agree to abide by our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
