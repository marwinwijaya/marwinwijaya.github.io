What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files or similar browser-based technologies stored on a user’s device when visiting a website. They help websites function efficiently, remember settings, improve usability, and support analytics.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie TypeTypical Purpose
Essential CookiesSupport page delivery, navigation, and basic security.
Performance CookiesMeasure website performance, traffic patterns, and interaction trends.
Functional CookiesRemember selected preferences or usability settings where available.
Analytics CookiesProvide aggregate insight into how visitors discover and use the website.

Purpose of Cookies

  • Support reliable page loading and technical stability.
  • Understand which pages or content areas are most frequently accessed.
  • Measure engagement trends and improve site usability.
  • Inform future content and feature decisions.

Third-Party Cookies

The website may use third-party services such as analytics, embedded content, infrastructure tools, or performance monitoring platforms. Those services may set or access cookies in accordance with their own terms and privacy notices.

How Users Can Manage Cookies

Most browsers allow users to manage cookie preferences through browser settings. Users can often review stored cookies, clear them manually, block certain cookies, or configure alerts when websites attempt to set them.

Blocking essential cookies may affect parts of website functionality, performance, or user experience.

Updates to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated to reflect changes in site functionality, analytics practices, or broader privacy operations. Users should review this page periodically for the latest version.
