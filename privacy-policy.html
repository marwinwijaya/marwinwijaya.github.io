Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how marwinwijaya.ggfsystem.com may collect, use, store, and protect personal information when visitors browse the website, submit inquiries, or interact with analytics and cookies.

ScopeWebsite, forms, analytics, and business communications
Contactmuhamad.arwinwijaya@gg-foods.om

Introduction

Protecting personal information is part of maintaining a professional and trustworthy website. This page describes baseline privacy practices in general terms and should be read together with any project-specific forms or notices shown to users.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details voluntarily submitted through forms or email, such as name, company, email address, and message content.
  • Professional inquiry details, service interests, or project information that users choose to provide.
  • Technical usage data such as IP address, browser type, device information, page views, referral sources, and timestamps.
  • Cookie and analytics data used to understand traffic, improve website performance, and measure engagement.

How We Use Information

  • Respond to messages, consultations, collaboration requests, or service inquiries.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve website content, usability, and technical reliability.
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and support security reviews.
  • Analyze aggregate engagement trends to improve content and user experience.

Data Storage and Retention

Information may be stored using reputable hosting, email, analytics, and productivity platforms. Data is generally retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for communication, recordkeeping, operational review, security purposes, or applicable obligations, then deleted, anonymized, or archived as appropriate.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

Information is not sold as part of ordinary website operations. Limited sharing may occur with service providers that support hosting, analytics, communications, security, or maintenance, or where disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect rights, investigate misuse, or respond to lawful requests.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The website may use cookies, analytics scripts, browser storage, and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure traffic, and support functionality. Additional details are available in the Cookie Policy.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards may be used to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. Users should avoid sending highly sensitive information through standard web forms unless a secure channel has been clearly established.
